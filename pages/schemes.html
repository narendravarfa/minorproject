Agriculture
PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Provides income support of ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments to all landholding farmer families across the country.

Benefits:
  • ₹6,000 yearly bank cash transfer.
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) security.
Eligibility:
  • Small/marginal landholding farmers.
  • Land ownership papers in applicant's name.

Housing & Infra
PM Awas Yojana (PMAY-Gramin)

Financial subsidy program to help homeless families or families living in dilapidated houses build concrete (pucca) housing units.

Benefits:
  • Up to ₹1.20 Lakh subsidy for plain areas.
  • Additional subsidy support for toilet building.
Eligibility:
  • Families without pucca home units.
  • SECC data verified under poverty lists.
Official Portal
Labour & Wage
MGNREGA Job Card Scheme

Guarantees 100 days of manual wage employment in every financial year to adult members of rural households willing to do volunteer unskilled manual work.

Benefits:
  • 100 days guaranteed wage labor.
  • Direct bank wage payment within 15 days.
Eligibility:
  • Adult rural family members.
  • Willing to perform unskilled manual activities.
Official Portal
Health & Care
Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Provides health insurance coverage up to ₹5 Lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization across public/private networks.

Benefits:
  • ₹5,00,000 cashless medical insurance.
  • Covers diagnostics, medicine, pre/post-hospital care.
Eligibility:
  • Identified vulnerable rural families.
  • No age, size, or gender limit exclusions.
Official Portal
Housing & Infra
Jal Jeevan Mission (Har Ghar Jal)

Aims to provide safe and adequate drinking water through individual household tap connections to all households in rural India.

Benefits:
  • 55 liters water per person daily tap connection.
  • Water quality testing and purification training.
Eligibility:
  • Every rural family household.
  • Joint implementation via village committee.
Official Portal
Pension
Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension

A monthly financial pension benefit scheme to elderly, widow, and disabled citizens belonging to Below Poverty Line (BPL) lists.

Benefits:
  • Up to ₹500 - ₹1000 monthly cash deposits.
  • Direct disbursement on 1st of every month.
Eligibility:
  • Age 60 years and above.
  • BPL database verified residents.
Official Portal
