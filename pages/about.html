Vision, Mission & Goals

Strategic directions directing the smart administrative systems of our local Panchayat office.

Our Vision

To transform Ramnagar into a fully digitized, carbon-neutral, model village that provides sustainable livelihood opportunities, clean environment, and digital convenience to all families.

Our Mission

Empower citizens through transparent, paperless administrative actions, modern public school education, clean public tap water networks, robust sanitation, and customized agricultural guidance.

Core Objectives

Achieve 100% literacy, provide 24/7 clean solar power on streets, enhance farming revenues via crop price optimizations, and support small-scale local handloom and cottage enterprises.

Our Journey & Timeline

A look back at the historical milestones and achievements that have shaped Ramnagar village over the years.

1952
Establishment of Panchayat

The local Gram Panchayat of Ramnagar was established, forming the first committee of five elected panches to manage agricultural dispute resolutions and primary roads.

1978
First Government School

Opened our first primary educational complex. This laid down the foundation for modern education, improving literacy across neighboring clusters.

2005
Clean Water Network Expansion

Commissioned our first localized overhead water tank and tap pipelines, reducing dependence on open wells and improving health standards.

2021
Transformed into a Smart Village

Adopted grid solar street lights, smart classroom panels in high schools, fully concrete internal streets, and complete digitized portal support.

Smart Gram Panchayat

Building transparent, digitally-connected local governance systems. Providing central utility files, village developments, and agricultural rates transparently.
