Village Infrastructure & Assets

A mapping of critical institutions, infrastructure developments, utilities, and social amenities in Ramnagar.

Education Institutions

1 Govt Higher Secondary Smart School, 2 Primary schools, and 3 Anganwadi early care child learning units.

Healthcare Centers

1 Primary Health Center (PHC) with 24/7 emergency nursing staff and a weekly visiting doctor team.

Financial Facilities

State Bank of India (SBI) Branch, cooperative agro-credit bank, and 2 ATM outlets.

Water Networks

100% tap-water penetration under Jal Jeevan, filtration wells, and solar-operated water pumps.

Electricity & Solar

99% domestic connection rates with smart digital meters. 150+ high-power solar LED street lights.

Internet Connectivity

High-speed optical fiber nodes under BharatNet. 4G/5G mobile tower backups from major telecom operators.

Smart Gram Panchayat

Building transparent, digitally-connected local governance systems. Providing central utility files, village developments, and agricultural rates transparently.
